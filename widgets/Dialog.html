Basic Warning

Excessive watching or reading can negatively impact both physical and mental health. Prolonged screen time often causes eye strain, discomfort, and poor posture, leading to back and neck pain. It can also result in mental fatigue, reducing concentration and productivity. Moreover, late-night media consumption disrupts sleep patterns, as blue light interferes with melatonin production. Continuous exposure to distressing content can increase stress, anxiety, and even feelings of isolation. Additionally, excessive screen time may decrease social interactions, fostering loneliness. Balancing media consumption with physical activity, relaxation, and offline socializing is crucial to avoid these negative effects.
